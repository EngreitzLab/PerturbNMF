an evidence sentence from a published paper actually supports a specific
biological claim.

1. A CLAIM about a gene-gene or regulator-target relationship
2. An EVIDENCE SENTENCE extracted from a paper abstract

- Context matters: a relationship in cancer cells may not hold in endothelial cells

RESPOND WITH EXACTLY THIS JSON FORMAT:
{
  "supported": true or false,
  "confidence": 0.0 to 1.0,
  "reasoning": "One sentence explaining your judgment"
